What makes it special

No Account Required

Play Minecraft without needing a Microsoft account. Add offline accounts directly and launch the full game.

Custom Skins

Save and load skins locally for offline accounts. Your skin shows in singleplayer automatically.

Quick Launch

One-click launch of your last played instance. No need to search through your modpacks.

Performance Presets

Auto-detects your system RAM and sets the optimal memory allocation. Perfect for low-end PCs.

Mod Management

Install mods from CurseForge, Modrinth, and more. Update, manage, and organize with ease.

Instance Backup

Backup your worlds, mods, and settings as a zip file. Restore anytime.

Discord Rich Presence

Show your friends what you're playing. Displays instance name and Minecraft version.

Crash Reports

Quick access to crash logs. Understand what went wrong and fix it fast.

Custom Themes

Dark, light, blue, and colored themes. Custom tooltips and menus that match your style.

Minimize to Tray

Keep the launcher running in the background. Close to tray, not exit.

Auto-Launch

Automatically launch your last played instance when the launcher starts.

Resolution Presets

Quick presets for 720p, 1080p, 1440p, and 4K. Or set your custom resolution.
